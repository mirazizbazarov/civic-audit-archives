# FINANCIAL AUDIT DATA
## "WHERE IS THE MONEY?"

> **Objective:** Contrast NGO asset holdings with their refusal to provide emergency grants.

### CONTENTS

1. **CPJ_Asset_Analysis.md**
   * **Finding:** Committee to Protect Journalists holds **$33.1M** in net assets.
   * **Metric:** Reserves cover 2.79 years of operating expenses.
   * **Grant Ratio:** Only ~6% of expenses go to direct grants.
   * **Verdict:** Asset Hoarding.

2. **RSF_Admin_Cost_Breakdown.md**
   * **Finding:** Reporters Without Borders (Paris) spends **48.7%** of budget on Administrative & Fundraising costs.
   * **Verdict:** Administrative Bloat.
